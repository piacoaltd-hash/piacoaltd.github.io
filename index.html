About PIACOA Ltd

PIACOA Ltd is a small, independent consulting firm specializing in the coordination and support of cross-border legal matters.

PIACOA has extensive experience supporting international litigation and complex cross-border disputes, particularly involving Europe and Latin America.

We work primarily with European and Latin American law firms on complex litigation and multi-jurisdictional cases, providing:

  • Legal documentation management
  • Contextual and regulatory analysis
  • Liaison between international legal teams
  • Project-based interventions for sensitive or complex matters

PIACOA operates as a flexible, project-focused consultancy, ensuring confidentiality, precision, and timely delivery of legal support.

Areas of Expertise

  • Cross-border legal coordination
  • Strategic advisory in sensitive jurisdictions
  • Risk analysis & due diligence
  • Support for investigations and complex disputes
  • Interface between legal, corporate, and local actors
